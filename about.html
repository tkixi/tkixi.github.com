About Me

A snippet

I was born in Seoul, South Korea. Influenced by the American and Korean culture, I grew up to understand and adapt in different environments, politically and socially. I am currently majoring in Computer Science at Boston University.

Interests

I have been an advocate for photography and traveling for many years now. I believe the hardest step in my two interests is to take the first initiative. After the first step is taken, everything else falls into place. To extend my enthusiasm for photography even more, I have learned to utilize Adobe Photoshop CS6 to create illusions and manipulations.

Goals

From the onset of my undergraduate education, I desired to have a life changing experience by the time I graduate from Boston University. During my Freshman year, I played for Boston University's League of Legends team and competed in collegiate e-Sports and gained a scholarship from Riot Games. During my Sophomore year, I took part on the Boston University's Korean Student Association's Executive Board and have met Youtuber David So. Now for my Junior year, I take on the responsibility to succeed as Vice President of KSA and to secure an internship for the Summer of 2018.
